Skeleton Smasher!

The evil Necromancer has summoned hordes of skeletons from graveyards across the land to destroy the world! With your power of Clicking you must smash the undead and recruit an army to aid you in your battle against evil!

How to Play

  1. Click the button in the middle to smash skeletons!
  2. Recruit units to aid you in smashing the skeletons!
  3. Buy upgrades to enhance your smashing of skeletons!
  4. There is no step four! The smashing never ends!
